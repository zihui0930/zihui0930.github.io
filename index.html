About Me

Zihui Zhang is a PostDoc in vLAR Group led by Prof. Bo Yang at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He received Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Prof. Bo Yang (2021-2026). Previously, he received his M.Sc. degree from the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Huibin Li (2018-2021), and his B.Sc. degree from the School of Mathematical Science, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (2014-2018). His research interests lie in 3D unsupervised learning, 3D/4D scene generation and world models.
