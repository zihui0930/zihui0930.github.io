About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, advised by Prof. Bo Yang. My work focuses on unsupervised learning in 3D vision and 3D generative models, especially object-centric representations and segmentation in point clouds.

Research Interests

  • Unsupervised learning in 3D vision
  • 3D generative models
  • 3D semantic and instance segmentation
